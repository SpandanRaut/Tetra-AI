05 / QUESTIONS

The things we
get asked most.

Why no pricing on the site?

Because every project is different and a price tag without context is misleading. After a 30-minute call we send a one-page quote with a fixed number. If we can't help, we'll tell you that too.

How small is too small a project?

We take on small briefs all the time — a single landing page, a focused chatbot, a one-week automation sprint. If it's interesting and useful to you, it's worth a call.

Do you work with non-technical founders?

Constantly. You don't need to know what a vector database is. You need to know what you're trying to do for your customers — we'll handle the rest, and explain anything we build in plain English.

What about ongoing support?

Two weeks of post-launch tuning are always included. After that you can keep us on a small monthly retainer for monitoring and changes, or take the codebase and run with it — your choice.

Which AI models do you use?

Whichever fits — usually Claude (Anthropic), GPT (OpenAI), or open-weights models for local / on-prem builds. We pick based on the task, not what's trending this week.

Where are you based?

Fully remote and globally distributed. We work with clients across Europe, North America, Asia, and beyond. Calls happen on whatever timezone works for you.
